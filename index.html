Welcome to the GeoX Lab!

GeoX Lab aims to use and develop state-of-art GIScience theories and technologies to advance smart city for a better city life. A better understanding of the relationship and interaction between human mobility and urban environment is crucial to researchers, practitioners and policy makers, with applications ranging from public health and traffic forecasting to urban planning. The rise of Big Data has led to a new paradigm of urban computing and informatics. The research plan focuses on using spatiotemporal Big Data to build up an urban computing platform for the assessment of urban performance indexes.
